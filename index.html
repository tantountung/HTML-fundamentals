Will tourism ever bounce back after vaccination?

Restart tourism: bounce back or bounce forward?

2021 might be the year where we see the first signs of travel and tourism’s recovery. Societies have become adapted to social distancing, measures are more tailored to local situations and large-scale vaccination programs are in progress.

On the consumer side, various studies show that the intention to travel is remarkable strong. Moreover, consumers had little opportunity to consume resulting in a record high level of savings which they may possibility spend at large on tourism and travel as well.
